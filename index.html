Primary education:

It is an earliest level of complusory education which last for at least six to seven years. Kindergarten it is lower primary education which last for grade (1-3).Upper primary education last for grade (4-6)

Middle education:

Middle school is last for grade 6-8.Students attending middle school are around age 11 to 13.

Secondary education:

It began with 9 grade and end up with 10 grade and it is complusory.

Intermidiate:

It is the most important stage of education.In this stage students chose subject of there intrest.It consist of 11th and 12th grade.Its duration is 2 years.
